Cyclistic Rider Behavior Insights

Google Data Analytics Capstone Project

This case study explores how casual and annual members use Cyclistic’s bike-share service differently. I analyzed Q1 2020 Divvy trip data using SQL in BigQuery, performed data cleaning and transformation, and visualized the results in Tableau Public. The goal was to identify behavioral patterns that can help convert casual riders into paying members.

🔍 Key Findings:

  • Casual riders take longer, leisure-focused trips on weekends
  • Members ride during commute hours from downtown transit stations
  • Targeted campaigns can help convert casuals into members

✅ Recommendations:

  1. Offer weekend membership deals
  2. Use signage at tourist stations
  3. Highlight savings for weekday commuters
